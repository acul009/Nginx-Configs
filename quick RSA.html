Tutorial

Sending Messages

1) Ask the person you want to send a message to for their public key

2) Paste the public key into the upper textarea in the encryption section

3) Write your message into the lower textarea

4) Click on encrypt

5) Send the encrypted message to the recipient

Receiving Messages

1) Open the keypair settings and generate a key

2) Click on copy public key

3) Send the public key to the sender

4) Paste the encrypted message into the lower textarea in the encryption section

5) Click on decrypt
